My Journey in Becoming a Web Developr

Experience

Way back in the early 90's I started getting into this new fangled world wide web. Back in those days things were very simple. Alas life got in the way and I shelved my aspirations to become a web developer.

Now way later in life things have come full circle and well, here I am again trying to fill my head full of knowledge.

I feel the timing is right and the old saying it's never too late seems all the more true!

Interests

Video games, Dad stuff, outdoors. Tinkering with electonics. Paying the rent, keeping the lights on.

With all of that, it seems I barely have a dull moment.

I see web development as a life long pursuit with no finish line. There will always be emerging tech to learn and master.
